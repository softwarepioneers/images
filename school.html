Online Learning

Online learning is education that takes place over the Internet. It is often referred to as “e- learning” among other terms. However, online learning is just one type of “distance learning” - the umbrella term for any learning that takes place across distance and not in a traditional classroom.

Online Teaching

Online teaching is education that takes place over the Internet. It is often referred to as “e- teaching” among other terms. However, online teaching is just one type of “distance teaching” - the umbrella term for any teaching that takes place across distance and not in a traditional classroom.

Online Tutorial

An online tutorial is a self study activity designed to teach or learn a specific teaching outcome or learning outcome. They are usually delivered via internet but can also be made available via the campus or on a youtube channel. And they are recorded tutorial and live streaming tutorial.

Online Examinations

Online examination is an online test to measure the knowledge of the participants on a given topic. And auto corrected exam. In the olden days, everybody had to gather in a classroom at the same time to take an exam. With online examination students can do the exam online.

Online assignments

Online assignments also allow for an archive to be created of relevant course information and discussions. This information can be used by students at a later date while studying for a test, or by the instructor to create a frequently asked questions page or to measure students' understanding of concepts.


Online Classes

Online classes are typically a mix of video recordings or live lectures supplemented with readings and assessments that students can complete on their own time by online. Online learning is most effective when delivered by teachers experienced in their subject matter.Using educational information technology by online through a computer or a mobile.
